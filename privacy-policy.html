Privacy Policy

This policy explains what limited data this site handles and how inquiries are managed.

By Elizabeth Ann Stein Effective February 18, 2026

1. Information collected

This website is primarily informational. We do not run account systems, user dashboards, or behavioral profiling tools. If you contact us through the email link or the contact form, the information you voluntarily share (such as your name, email address, and trip details) is used only to respond to your request.

2. How information is used

Inquiry details are used solely to provide local travel recommendations and follow-up communication related to your request. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We do not use your message content for advertising segmentation.

3. Cookies and analytics

This site does not currently run invasive tracking scripts or ad-tech integrations. Standard server logs may still record technical data such as IP address, browser type, and request timestamps for basic reliability and security monitoring.

If analytics tools are introduced in the future, this policy will be updated to describe what is collected, why it is collected, and how long data is retained. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at the top of this page.

4. Data retention

Contact information is retained only as long as reasonably needed to complete correspondence and provide requested recommendations. You can request deletion of prior inquiry messages by emailing hello@washingtonpa-guide.com.

5. Third-party links

This website may link to external destinations, museums, and local businesses. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites. Review their policies independently before sharing personal information.

6. Contact for policy questions

For questions about this policy or data handling practices, email hello@washingtonpa-guide.com.
