What to include in your request

The best recommendations come from clear constraints. Let me know who is traveling, how much time you have, and what kind of pace you enjoy. Some visitors want a packed day with multiple stops, while others want one or two highlights with plenty of unstructured time for coffee, conversation, and short neighborhood walks.

If anyone in your group has accessibility needs, include that early. I can prioritize routes and destinations that are easier to navigate. You can also mention whether weather backup plans are important. Western Pennsylvania weather can shift quickly, so indoor alternatives are often worth planning in advance.

Response target: within two business days. For same-weekend visits, include your exact date range so I can prioritize quickly.

I can also suggest a budget-friendly version of the same itinerary if you want to minimize paid attractions while still getting a strong sense of Washington County. If food is a priority, note dietary needs and whether you prefer quick casual spots or sit-down dinner pacing.

You are welcome to include transportation context as well. Visitors arriving by car usually have more flexibility, but I can still map compact walking-first options for travelers who want to stay near downtown and keep driving to a minimum.
